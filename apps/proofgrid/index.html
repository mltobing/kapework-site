Kapework · Proof Grid v1

How to Play

Fill the 4×4 grid so every row and column contains each of the 4 symbols exactly once:

Hollow Circle
Filled Circle
Hollow Square
Filled Square

Clue Strips

Row clues (left side) show the shape of each cell in that row — circle or square — but not whether it's hollow or filled.

Column clues (top) show the fill of each cell in that column — hollow or filled — but not whether it's a circle or square.

Combine both clue types to deduce the exact symbol for each cell.
