Kapework · Proof Grid 6×6

How to Play

Fill the 6×6 grid so every row and column contains all 6 symbols exactly once. Tap a cell to cycle through symbols.

Hollow Circle
Filled Circle
Hollow Square
Filled Square
Hollow Triangle
Filled Triangle

Clues sit between adjacent cells:

Same shape (both circles, both squares, or both triangles)
Same fill (both hollow or both filled)

No clue means unknown — it does not mean "different."

You have 1 check. The board must be fully filled before you can check.

Triangles are new — this is the hard mode. 6×6 is much deeper than 4×4.
