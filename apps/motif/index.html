How to Play

Fill the 4×4 grid so every row and column contains each symbol exactly once. Tap a cell to cycle through symbols.

Hollow Circle
Filled Circle
Hollow Square
Filled Square

Clues sit between adjacent cells:

Same shape (both circles or both squares)
Same fill (both hollow or both filled)

No clue means unknown — it does not mean "different."

You get 2 checks per puzzle. The board must be completely filled before you can check.
